WFP Lists Wesley Heights Stunner

Modern transformation of historic Tudor.

Washington Fine Properties (WFP) is pleased to announce the offering of 3001 Foxhall Road NW, showcasing the complete transformation of an original 1935 Tudor home into a stunning new residence with a modern interior design by 3G Architects. The home is listed for $6,996,995. 

Located in the prestigious Wesley Heights neighborhood, the home was completely gutted and expanded in 2023 – 2024. It features luminous rooms with high ceilings and large expanses of glass that flood the interior with light.  With almost 9,000 square feet of usable space, the home offers beautiful common areas, 6 bedrooms and 7.5 baths, a fabulous chef’s kitchen/family room combination, a heated saltwater swimming pool and a covered three-car garage that can be entered from a private drive off Hawthorne Street or a shared drive off Foxhall Road.  The floor-to-ceiling windows and multiple porches and balconies offer lovely views from almost every room and create an exciting interplay between indoor and outdoor spaces. 

Visitors step through the original turreted entry into a dramatic, multi-story foyer with views all the way through the house to the treetops beyond.  Ahead is a sizable reception room with a wet bar and wine refrigerator. The adjacent living room features a gas fireplace and opens to a well-proportioned dining room with a modern chandelier and a wall of windows overlooking the pool and neighborhood.

Extending across the back of the house, an extraordinary family room has floor-to-ceiling windows, another gas fireplace and a door leading down to the rear garden and pool. The chef’s kitchen, open to the family room, has everything cooks love: abundant well-designed storage and Ultralux cabinetry, Miele appliances including a 36” refrigerator and 36” freezer, multiple ovens and dishwashers, an espresso machine and wine refrigerator.  It opens to a family “command center”.  Tucked off to the side is a private first floor bedroom or office and full bath, with its own exterior entrance and terrace.

The second level primary suite offers a cozy sitting room with two skylights, a spacious bedroom with private balcony, a modern two-sided fireplace, a wet bar with mini refrigerator, and an exceptional walk-in closet.  The primary bath is sunny and sumptuous, with a large steam shower, custom double vanity, soaking tub with treetop views and a water closet. There are three additional bedrooms on this level – one with spacious balcony, one with a fireplace, all with stylish en suite baths. 

A lovely third floor loft space with roof access would be perfect as a home office, a yoga studio or just a quiet retreat from the hustle and bustle of the household. 

The home is an entertainer’s dream.  Its well- integrated lower level has guest quarters, a kitchenette with a refrigerator, dishwasher and oversized wine refrigerator (222 bottle capacity), a second laundry area, a game room, and a family room that leads out to a delightful covered cabana with a full bath and a heated, saltwater swimming pool with illuminated water features.

The rear garden and pool are fully fenced, and manicured landscaping offers varied plantings and a natural privacy hedge by the pool that will continue to grow and thicken. 

The home is meticulously constructed, with double and triple paned Pella windows that eliminate city noise, open and closed cell insulation, slate and TPO roofing, and stylish design choices. 

“The space, the interior light, the natural flow of the floorplan, the thoughtful amenities and superb craftsmanship set this home apart,” said Tammy Gale of Washington Fine Properties. “No detail has been overlooked by the design team.  We are very proud to be representing this extraordinary property.”
